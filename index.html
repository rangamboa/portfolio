about me

Hello! I'm Ran, a web developer. I'm a creative who's passionate about building websites.

The best sites are nice to look at, easy to navigate, simple to read and sometimes even fun to use. When a user returns repeatedly to accomplish the task at hand, I've done my job.

I make applications using a variety of methodologies and languages. Picking up a new way to build isn't a problem for me-- I love discovering novel ways to create. I enjoy developing apps on both the front- and back-end, then integrating the two to create a pleasant user experience.

Please have a look at my work below; they range from games to more practical applications. If you'd be interested in working with me, please send me a note! Thank you sincerely for your interest.
